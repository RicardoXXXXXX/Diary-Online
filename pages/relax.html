Reduce your stress

Writing down your feelings helps you to "brain-dump" your anxieties, frustrations and pains on a diary. This can help you to reduce and release any stress which you have accumulated overtime. A good way to relive stress is to write in the stream of consciousness style first thing in the morning called "Morning pages". You can also use the mood tracker found in Journey to indicate your sentiment level. Overall, expressing yourself in a diary is a good way to free up any tension that prevents you from feeling happy.
