My Hometown

My hometown is Hengyang City, Hunan Province. This is a city with a long history. It was built in the fifth year of Gaozu in the Western Han Dynasty (202 BC). Many famous historical buildings are located here. Such as Shigu Academy which is one of the four significant academies in ancient China, was built here in the fifth year of Tang Yuanhe (810). In addition, it also has the famous Taoist and Buddhist shrines in Nanyue Hengshan which is one of the five celebrated mountains in China. It is said that "the wild goose flies from the north to the south, and stops here (Hengyang) to rest its wings", so Hengyang is also called Yancheng (the City of Wild Goose). Today, Hengyang is a modern city with a population of several million. It is an important railway transportation hub in Hunan Province and is famous for its developed mining industry. Furthermore, there are many famous people from Hengyang as well, such as Ma Ying-jeou, former president of the Republic of China, Luo Ronghuan, one of the top ten founding marshals of the People's Republic of China, and Liu Qingsong (FPX Crisp), a member of the champion team (Funplus Phoenix) of the League of Legends 2019 Global Finals.
